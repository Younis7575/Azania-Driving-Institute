Azania Driving Institute's Proposed Partnership with North Link College

Azania Driving Institute (ADI) is excited to announce a potential partnership with North Link College, a leading educational institution with over 25,000 students. We are exploring the possibility of providing driver training to 5,000 North Link College students per year.

Key Elements of the Proposed Partnership:

  • Tailored Driver Training: ADI would offer a comprehensive program that includes learners course, simulator training, and practical road lessons.
  • Efficient Delivery: Our proven methodologies would ensure efficient training for a large student body.
  • Positive Outcomes: The partnership aims to equip students with essential driving skills, enhancing their opportunities and safety.

Benefits for North Link College:

  • Enhanced Student Services: Offering driver training would add value to the college's offerings.
  • Improved Student Outcomes: Equipped students would be better prepared for employment and independent living.
  • Cost-Effective Solution: ADI's efficient approach would make the partnership financially viable.

At ADI, we're more than just instructors; we're partners in your journey towards becoming a confident and responsible driver. We believe that every road leads to new adventures, and we're honoured to be a part of yours. With every lesson, we strive to instil not only driving skills but also a sense of confidence and independence. Thank you for choosing ADI. Let's hit the road together.
